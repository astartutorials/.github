## What we do

A-Star Tutorials connects university students with qualified tutors for **group and private sessions** across a wide range of courses. Whether you need a last-minute push before an exam or consistent support throughout the semester, we match you with the right tutor at the right time.

We're a small, focused team building tools that make academic support more **accessible**, more **personal**, and a lot less stressful.
